SWRL rules

  • Rule 1 - Class III devices are high risk devices
  • Rule 2 - Manufacturers manufacture their devices
  • Rule 3 - Critical risks generate high priority incidents

Rule 1 - Class III devices are high risk devicesback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If a device has risk class III, then it is inferred as a high risk device.

Device1 c(?d) , has risk class op(?d,?RiskClass_III) -> High Risk Device c(?d)

Rule 2 - Manufacturers manufacture their devicesback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If a device has a manufacturer, the inverse manufacturing relation can be inferred.

Device1 c(?d) , has manufacturer op(?d,?m) -> manufactures device op(?m,?d)

Rule 3 - Critical risks generate high priority incidentsback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If an incident is related to a critical risk, it is inferred as a high priority incident.

Incident c(?i) , related to risk op(?i,?r) , Critical Risk c(?r) -> High Priority Incident c(?i)
